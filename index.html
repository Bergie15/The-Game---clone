The Game

A cooperative card game. Play all 98 cards onto the four piles before the deck and your hands run dry.

How to Play

  • There are four piles: two ascending piles starting at 1, and two descending piles starting at 100.
  • On an ascending pile, you must play a card higher than the top card. On a descending pile, you must play a card lower than the top card.
  • The 10 trick: you may play a card exactly 10 lower on an ascending pile, or exactly 10 higher on a descending pile — sending that pile back the "wrong" way.
  • On your turn you must play at least 2 cards (only 1 if the draw pile is empty).
  • After playing, draw back up to your hand limit from the deck (if any cards remain).
  • Hand size: 8 cards for 1–2 players, 7 for 3 players, 6 for 4–5 players.
  • You win together if all 98 cards get played. You lose if a player cannot legally play the minimum required cards.
  • Since you can't show your hand to teammates, talk in vague terms only ("I have something close to 60") — no exact numbers!
