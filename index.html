The Game

A cooperative climb. Empty your hands onto four piles — two counting up from 1, two counting down from 100 — before the deck runs dry.

How to Play

  • There are four piles: two ascending piles starting at 1, and two descending piles starting at 100.
  • On an ascending pile, play a card higher than the top card. On a descending pile, play a card lower than the top card.
  • The 10 trick: play a card exactly 10 lower on an ascending pile, or exactly 10 higher on a descending pile — sending that pile back the "wrong" way.
  • Each turn, play at least 2 cards (just 1 once the deck is empty).
  • After playing, draw back up to your hand limit from the deck.
  • Hand size: 8 cards for 1–2 players, 7 for 3, 6 for 4–5.
  • Win together if all 98 cards get played. Lose if a player can't legally play the minimum.
  • You can't show your hand — describe it only in vague terms ("something near 60").
